Areca-Nuts Plates Bowls and Spoon are the disposable, multipurpose plates bowls and Spoon. First of all the rinds of the Areca-Nut plants is collected and they are rinsed with fresh water and dried for few days and are heated at certain temperature and pressed with the Keys shaping them diffrent items. And they are cut in accordance to the required shape . Then after their edges are trimmed and given a fixed shape thenafter they are cleaned and polished and send to market. These a plates,bowls casn be used for many occasions, including religious rituals, and can be commercially used . The Areca-Nut utensils are mostly used for picnics, restaurant use, village parties, and at the street food stalls as a disposable plates and can also be used at homes for indoor use.

 

Areca-Nut Plates is a slightly curved plate bigger than bowls in diffrent shape made by heating the rinds of Areca-Nut plants and giving diffrent shape and size of usual plates of metal. The Rind is dry and brown in color. After the plate is given the final touch we can serve dishes in it and is water resistant. It is liquid resistant means leackage is not possible .

 

Areca-Nut Bowls is a medium-size bowl, either circular or rectangular and can be used to hold semi-liquid objects. Made as the similar process involved in preparing the plates

 

Areca-Nut Spoon/Fork is attractive reusable and cheaper spoon alike usual metal spoon made from rinds of Areca-Nut Plant

 

These rinds are handpicked from the dry tree of Areca-Nuts , however, some fresh rinds are also collected from the ground. These rinds are sacked together and they are rinsed with fresh water and dried for few days and are heated at certain temperature and pressed with the Keys shaping them diffrent items. And they are cut in accordance to the required shape . Then after their edges are trimmed and given a fixed shape thenafter they are cleaned and polished and send to market.
